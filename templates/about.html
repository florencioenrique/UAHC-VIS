Vehicle Identification System

University of Antique - Hamtic Campus: Elevating campus safety through intelligent QR integration and real-time monitoring.

Mission & Purpose

The UAHC-VIS aims to provide an efficient and secure way to track and identify vehicles on campus. Each vehicle is assigned a unique QR code that, when scanned, displays essential details instantly.

Entry/Exit Logs
Automated tracking for total transparency.
Access Control
Prevent unauthorized entry effectively.

Key Capabilities

Intelligent Scanning

Instant retrieval of owner details and vehicle specs via unique encrypted QR codes.

Real-time Logs

Live monitoring of vehicle movement across campus perimeters for enhanced security response.

Unified Admin Hub

A central command center for reporting violations and managing campus-wide traffic.

System Stakeholders

Tailored interfaces for every member of the University.

General Users

Identify vehicles via activities.

Staff/Faculty

Manage personal vehicle assets.

Security Team

Monitor perimeter access.

Administrators

Full system governance.

Security & Data Privacy

We prioritize data integrity. Only essential information (Plate No., Contact, Address) is retrieved to ensure compliance with privacy standards while maintaining operational security.

Our Vision

UAHC-VIS is an evolving ecosystem. Our roadmap includes AI-driven plate recognition and deeper integration with campus emergency protocols.
